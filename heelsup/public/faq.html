Orders & Delivery

Do you deliver all over India?
Yes, we deliver across India with safe packaging and reliable shipping partners to ensure your order reaches you smoothly.
How long does delivery take?
Orders are usually delivered within 3–7 business days depending on your location. Jodhpur orders might arrive even faster!
How can I track my order?
Once your order is shipped, you will receive tracking details via WhatsApp, SMS, or email. You can also track it directly from the "Track Order" page in your Profile.
Do you offer Cash on Delivery (COD)?
Yes, Cash on Delivery (COD) is available on eligible orders above ₹2000 depending on location and service availability. Additional convenience charges may apply on COD orders.

Products & Sizing

Are your products premium quality?
Absolutely. Every product is carefully selected and quality-checked to ensure comfort, durability, and premium finishing. 21.7K+ customers trust us!
How do I choose the correct size?
We recommend checking the size chart available on each product page before placing your order. We generally stock standard India sizes 3-9. If you are between sizes or need assistance, our support team on WhatsApp will be happy to guide you.
Do you have wedding and party wear collections?
Yes, we offer a wide range of premium wedding heels, party wear footwear, elegant flats, and stylish bags for every occasion. Check out our Wedding Collection tab from the menu!

Returns & Exchange

What is your Return Policy?
You can return or exchange items within 7 days of delivery. The product must be unused, in its original condition, and with all tags attached. Please read our full Return Policy page for details.
What if the size doesn’t fit me?
If you face a size issue, simply request an exchange for eligible products within the allowed exchange period by reaching out on WhatsApp. We will arrange a pickup and send the right size!
What if I receive a damaged or wrong product?
Immediately send us photos or an unboxing video on WhatsApp within 48 hours of delivery. We will arrange a replacement for damaged or wrong products within 24 hours at no extra charge.
How long does a refund take?
Once we receive and inspect the returned product, the refund is processed within 5-7 business days to your original payment method. UPI and Wallet refunds are typically faster.

Payments

Which payment methods do you accept?
We accept UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), Credit Cards, Debit Cards, Net Banking, Wallets, and Cash on Delivery (COD) for a safe checkout experience.
Is EMI available?
Yes! For orders above ₹1999, EMI options (3, 6, 12 months) are available on major credit cards during the checkout process.
How do I use a discount code?
On the checkout page, look for the "Apply Coupon" field. Enter your valid code and click Apply. The discount will be automatically deducted from your total.
