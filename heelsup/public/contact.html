Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take?
Standard delivery takes 5–7 business days pan-India. Express (2–3 days) and Same-Day (Jodhpur only) options are also available. You will receive a tracking link via SMS/Email within 24 hours of placing your order.
What is your exchange policy?
We offer a 7-day hassle-free exchange policy. Please ensure the product is unused and in its original packaging. Contact our support team via WhatsApp to initiate the process, and we will arrange a smooth pickup for exchange.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major Credit/Debit Cards, Net Banking, and UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm) through our secure payment gateway. EMI options are also available on orders above ₹1,999.
