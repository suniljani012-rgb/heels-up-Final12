Common Questions

Shipping FAQs

Answers to the most frequently asked questions — straight and simple.

When do I get free shipping?

Shipping is completely FREE on every order of ₹799 or more. No coupon code needed — it applies automatically at checkout.

How to track my order?

After dispatch, you will receive a tracking link via WhatsApp and SMS. Alternatively, go to the Order Tracking Page and enter your Order ID for real-time status.

Is COD (Cash on Delivery) available?

Currently, Cash on Delivery (COD) is temporarily disabled as we upgrade our logistics. You can place your orders securely using UPI, Credit/Debit Cards, or Net Banking.

In how many days will the order be delivered?

Standard delivery takes 5-7 business days. If you select Express, it will arrive in 2-3 days. Same-day delivery is available in Jodhpur city (if ordered before 12 PM).

Delivery failed — what to do now?

The courier attempts delivery 3 times. After failed attempts, the parcel is held at the nearest courier hub for 5-7 days. on WhatsApp — we will arrange re-delivery.

Packaging is damaged or product is missing?

In such cases, be sure to make an unboxing video at the time of delivery — and report to us within 24 hours. We will immediately process a replacement or refund. Your satisfaction is our priority.

Will multiple items come in one order or separately?

Generally, they arrive in one package. If items are shipped from different warehouses, they might arrive separately — but you will receive separate tracking links for both.

Is international delivery available?

Currently we deliver only in India. We will be launching soon in the UAE, UK, and US. Subscribe to our newsletter — we'll notify you first upon launch!
