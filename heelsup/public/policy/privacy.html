📋 Summary: HeelsUp ("hum", "humari company") aapki personal information ki privacy ko seriously leta hai. Yeh policy explain karti hai ki hum kya data collect karte hain, kyun collect karte hain, aur aap apna data kaise control kar sakte hain. Agar koi sawaal ho — hume directly WhatsApp karo.

1 Introduction

Yeh Privacy Policy HeelsUp (heelsup.in) dwara collect ki jaane wali information ko describe karti hai jab aap hamari website visit karte hain ya hamse koi product purchase karte hain. Is policy ko carefully read karo taaki samjho ki hum aapki information kaise handle karte hain.

Hamari website use karke aap is Privacy Policy ki terms se agree karte hain. Agar aap agree nahi karte, please hamari website use nahi karein.

2 Data We Collect

Hum different types ki information collect karte hain:

Personal Information

Naam, email address, phone number, delivery address — jo aap account create karte waqt ya order place karte waqt provide karte hain.

Payment Information

Payment details encrypted form mein process hoti hain. Hum aapke full card numbers store nahi karte — yeh secure payment gateways (Razorpay, Paytm) handle karte hain.

Usage Data

Browser type, IP address, pages visited, time spent — anonymous analytics data jo website improve karne ke liye use hoti hai.

Communication Data

WhatsApp messages, emails, aur customer support conversations — better service provide karne ke liye.

3 How We Use Your Data

Aapka data inke liye use hota hai:

  • Orders process karna aur delivery coordinate karna
  • Account manage karna aur password reset support dena
  • Order updates, shipping notifications aur delivery alerts bhejana
  • Customer service aur return/exchange queries handle karna
  • New arrivals, sale offers aur discount codes share karna (opt-in)
  • Website experience personalize karna
  • Fraud prevention aur security maintain karna
  • Legal obligations fulfill karna (GST, financial records)

4 Data Sharing

Hum aapka data third parties ko sell nahi karte. Data sirf in circumstances mein share hota hai:

  • Delivery Partners: Delhivery, BlueDart, DTDC — delivery complete karne ke liye naam aur address share hota hai
  • Payment Gateways: Razorpay, Paytm — secure payment processing ke liye
  • Analytics Services: Google Analytics — anonymous website traffic data
  • Legal Requirements: Court orders ya government requests par (jo applicable Indian law ke under ho)

🔒 Hum promise karte hain: Aapka personal data kabhi bhi advertisers, data brokers, ya unrelated third parties ko share nahi kiya jaayega. Aapki information sirf aapki order complete karne ke liye use hoti hai.

5 Cookies

Hamari website cookies use karti hai — small files jo aapke browser mein save hoti hain. Yeh cart, wishlist aur login session maintain karne ke liye important hain.

  • Essential Cookies: Cart aur session data — website properly kaam nahi karegi bina inke
  • Analytics Cookies: Google Analytics — website traffic samajhne ke liye (anonymous)
  • Marketing Cookies: Facebook Pixel — relevant ads dikhane ke liye (opt-out available)

Browser settings se aap cookies disable kar sakte hain, lekin isse kuch website features kaam karna band ho sakte hain.

6 Data Security

Aapka data secure rakhne ke liye hum yeh measures use karte hain:

  • SSL/TLS encryption — website aur servers ke beech data transfer ke liye
  • Secure payment processing through PCI-DSS compliant gateways
  • Regular security audits aur vulnerability checks
  • Limited staff access — sirf zaruri employees aapka data dekh sakte hain
  • Data breach notification — agar koi breach ho to hum immediately inform karenge

Note: Internet par 100% security guarantee nahi di ja sakti. Hum best practices follow karte hain lekin data transmission inherently risky hai.

7 Your Rights

Indian data protection laws ke under, aapke yeh rights hain:

👁️

Access

Hum se request kar sakte hain ki humne aapke baare mein kya data collect kiya hai.

✏️

Correction

Galat ya incomplete information correct/update kara sakte hain.

🗑️

Deletion

Account aur data delete karne ki request kar sakte hain (legal obligations ke subject to).

📵

Opt-Out

Marketing emails aur notifications se unsubscribe kar sakte hain anytime.

📦

Portability

Apna data machine-readable format mein export karne ki request kar sakte hain.

🚫

Object

Kisi specific purpose ke liye data processing object kar sakte hain.

In rights exercise karne ke liye hello@heelsup.in par email karo ya WhatsApp karo. Hum 30 days mein respond karenge.

8 Children's Privacy

HeelsUp 13 years se kam umra ke children ka data knowingly collect nahi karta. Hamari services adults aur teenagers (13+) ke liye designed hain.

Agar aap parent ya guardian hain aur believe karte hain ki aapke child ka data collect hua hai — please immediately hello@heelsup.in par contact karo. Hum promptly delete karenge.

9 Policy Changes

Hum is Privacy Policy ko update kar sakte hain jab zarurat ho. Major changes ke liye:

  • Website par notice post kiya jaayega
  • Registered users ko email notification bheja jaayega
  • Effective date update ki jaayegi (page ke top par)

Changes ke baad bhi website use karna automatically updated policy accept maana jaata hai.

Privacy Concerns?

Koi bhi privacy related sawaal ya complaint hai? Hum seriously lete hain aur 48 hours mein respond karte hain.
