Exchange Only Policy

HeelsUp operates on a strict **Exchange Only** policy. Since our premium ladies footwear and bags are handcrafted with the finest materials and utmost care, **we do not offer returns or refunds under any circumstances**. We are happy to help you exchange your item for a different size, color, or style within 7 days.

1 Overview

Our Exchange Policy ensures you find the absolute perfect fit and style. Since footwear and bags sizing can sometimes vary, we offer a seamless size or product exchange to keep you stepping in confidence.

This policy applies to all products purchased on heelsup.in. Products bought during clearance sales or specifically marked as non-exchangeable are excluded.

2 Exchange Eligibility

Exchange requests are accepted under the following conditions:

  • The request is initiated within 7 calendar days from the date of delivery.
  • The product is in **unused, unworn, and original condition** with no outdoor wear marks.
  • All original packaging (box, dust bag, tags, tissues) is completely intact.
  • The package includes the original purchase invoice or receipt.
  • A valid exchange reason (such as size adjustment or color swap) is provided.
Valid Exchange Reasons
  • Incorrect size delivered or size adjustments needed
  • Wrong product or wrong color delivered by mistake
  • Manufacturing defects (stitching, sole bonding, clasp, etc.)
  • Damaged during transit

3 Non-Exchangeable Items

The following items cannot be exchanged:

  • Requests raised after the 7-day exchange window has closed.
  • Items showing clear signs of outdoor wear, dirt, or usage.
  • Clearance Sale items (specifically marked as "Non-Exchangeable").
  • Custom-made, bespoke, or personalized orders.
  • Hygiene products or foot accessories like insoles, heel liners, and socks.
Important Note

If an item is returned for exchange and fails our quality check (e.g., shows signs of wear, scuffing, or missing tags), the exchange request will be rejected, and the item will be shipped back to you at your expense.

4 Exchange Process — Step by Step

Exchanging your item is simple. Just follow these 4 steps:

Step 1 — Reach Out

Contact our support team on WhatsApp at +91 9000000000 or email us at hello@heelsup.in. Provide your Order ID, size swap request, and product images.

Step 2 — Approval

Our team will verify the details and approve your exchange request within 24-48 hours.

Step 3 — Pickup & Inspection

Once approved, we will arrange a reverse pickup (free in Jodhpur, nominal reverse shipping charge of ₹99 applies for other locations). Pack the product securely in its original box.

Step 4 — New Dispatch

Upon receiving the product at our warehouse and passing quality inspection, we will dispatch your exchange item within 48 hours.

5 No Refunds Policy

We maintain a strict No Refunds policy. We do not issue cash refunds, UPI reversals, or card chargebacks for any orders. Instead, we offer:

  • Size or color exchanges for the same product.
  • Exchange for any other product of equal value.
  • If exchanging for a product of higher value, you only pay the price difference.
  • If exchanging for a lower value product, the difference will be issued as a HeelsUp store credit coupon valid for 6 months.

6 Damaged / Wrong Items Received

If you receive a damaged, defective, or incorrect product, we will replace it immediately at no additional cost:

  • Take clear photos/videos of the package and item upon delivery and send them to us within 24 hours.
  • We will arrange a priority free replacement pickup and dispatch the correct item.
  • An unboxing video is highly recommended to expedite claims for transit damages.
