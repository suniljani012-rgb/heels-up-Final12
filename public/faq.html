Orders & Delivery

Do you deliver all over India?
Yes, we deliver across India with safe packaging and reliable shipping partners to ensure your order reaches you smoothly.
How long does delivery take?
Orders are usually delivered within 3–7 business days depending on your location.
How can I track my order?
Once your order is shipped, you will receive tracking details via WhatsApp, SMS, or email. You can also track it directly from the "Track Order" page on our website.
Do you offer Cash on Delivery (COD)?
Yes, Cash on Delivery (COD) is available on eligible orders above ₹2000 depending on location and service availability. Additional convenience charges may apply on COD orders.

Products & Quality

Are your products premium quality?
Absolutely. Every product is carefully selected and quality-checked to ensure comfort, durability, and premium finishing.
Are the heels comfortable for long wear?
Yes, our collections are designed to balance both style and comfort, making them suitable for parties, weddings, and long wear.
Do the product images match the actual product?
Yes, we try our best to ensure product images accurately represent the real product. Slight color variations may occur due to screen settings and lighting.
Do you have wedding and party wear collections?
Yes, we offer a wide range of premium wedding heels, party wear footwear, elegant flats, and stylish bags for every occasion. Check out our Wedding Collection tab!
How do I choose the correct size?
We recommend checking the size chart available on each product page before placing your order. If you are between sizes or need assistance, our support team will be happy to guide you.
What if the size doesn’t fit me?
If you face a size issue, you can request an exchange for eligible products within the allowed exchange period. The product must be unused and returned with original packaging.

Returns & Support

Do you offer returns or refunds?
No, we currently do not offer returns or refunds. We only provide exchanges for eligible cases such as size issues, damaged products, or wrong items received.
What if I receive a damaged or wrong product?
If you receive a damaged or incorrect item, please contact our support team within 48 hours of delivery along with product images and a proper parcel opening video for verification.
Is parcel opening video compulsory for exchange requests?
Yes, a clear and unedited parcel opening video is mandatory for claims related to damaged, missing, or wrong products. Without the video, exchange requests may not be approved.
Can I exchange my order?
Yes, exchanges are available for eligible products. Once we receive and inspect the returned product, the exchange process may take around 7 business days or more depending on availability and logistics.
How can I contact HeelsUp support?
You can connect with us through WhatsApp, Instagram, email, or the Contact Us page on our website. Our team is always happy to assist you!
